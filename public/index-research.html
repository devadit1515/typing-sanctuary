Typing Sanctuary

🔐 Keystroke Biometric Authentication RESEARCH

Advanced security through typing pattern recognition - A research project exploring continuous authentication using keystroke dynamics

🎯 What is This?

This is a research version of Typing Sanctuary that implements keystroke biometric authentication - a cutting-edge security technology that identifies users based on their unique typing patterns.

Just like your fingerprint, the way you type is unique to you. This system analyzes timing patterns between keystrokes to create a "typing fingerprint" that can detect unauthorized access in real-time.

🔬 Research Goals

Study continuous authentication methods • Explore behavioral biometrics • Develop ML models for security • Privacy-preserving authentication

✨ Key Features

  • Continuous Authentication: Verifies your identity throughout your session, not just at login
  • Keystroke Dynamics: Analyzes dwell time, flight time, and typing rhythm
  • Machine Learning: Statistical models detect anomalies in typing patterns
  • Privacy-First: Only stores timing patterns, never the actual content you type
  • Real-Time Detection: Flags suspicious activity within 30 seconds
  • Research Platform: Contribute to academic research on biometric security

🎮 How It Works

Step 1: Give consent for keystroke data collection

Step 2: Play 5 typing games to build your biometric profile

Step 3: Every subsequent game verifies your typing pattern

Step 4: System flags anomalies if someone else uses your account

🔒 Your Privacy

We collect: Timing data (how long you hold keys, time between keystrokes)
We DON'T collect: The actual words or content you type
You control: All data with opt-out and deletion options at any time
