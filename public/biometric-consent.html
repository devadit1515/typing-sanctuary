🔐 Keystroke Biometric Authentication

Enhanced Security Through Typing Pattern Recognition

What is Keystroke Biometric Authentication?

Keystroke biometric authentication is a cutting-edge security technology that identifies you based on your unique typing patterns. Just like your fingerprint, the way you type—including the timing between keystrokes and how long you hold down each key—is unique to you.

How it works: As you play typing games, our system analyzes your typing rhythm, speed patterns, and keystroke dynamics to create a unique "typing fingerprint." This adds an extra layer of security to your account without requiring any additional effort from you!

Benefits

  • Enhanced Security: Protects your account from unauthorized access, even if someone knows your password
  • Continuous Authentication: Verifies your identity throughout your session, not just at login
  • No Extra Effort: Works automatically while you play—no additional passwords or codes needed
  • Session Hijacking Prevention: Detects if someone else tries to use your account mid-session
  • Privacy-Focused: Only stores statistical patterns, not the actual words you type

What Data is Collected?

We collect the following anonymized data:

  • Timing Metrics: Time between keystrokes (flight time) and key press duration (dwell time)
  • Rhythm Patterns: Your typing speed variations and pause patterns
  • Error Patterns: How and where you make typing errors
  • Device Information: Keyboard type and browser to adjust for different devices

Important: We do NOT record the actual content of what you type—only the timing patterns. Your privacy is protected!

How Your Data is Protected

  • Encrypted Storage: All biometric data is encrypted and stored securely
  • Limited Access: Only authorized systems can access your biometric profile
  • Anonymized Processing: Your typing patterns are converted to statistical features
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of all your biometric data at any time
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your biometric data is never sold or shared with third parties

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your biometric data:

  • Opt out at any time from your profile settings
  • Request a copy of your biometric profile
  • Delete all your biometric data permanently
  • View your authentication history
  • Update your consent preferences
