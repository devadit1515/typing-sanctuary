🔒 Your Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
You have the right to access, export, and delete your personal data at any time. Exported data includes all biometric information we've collected. To permanently delete your data, visit your profile settings.
📚 Research Data Usage
If you've consented to research use, your anonymized data may be included in academic publications and datasets. You can revoke research consent at any time from your profile settings, though previously published anonymized data cannot be retroactively removed.
